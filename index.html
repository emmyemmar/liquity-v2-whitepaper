1. Introduction and Motivation

Liquity V2 is a decentralized borrowing protocol on Ethereum that builds on the success of V1 by introducing dynamic interest rates, BOLD (a USD-pegged stablecoin), and support for liquid staking tokens (LSTs) as collateral. V2 enhances capital efficiency with a 110% minimum collateral ratio (vs. 150% in V1), enabling up to 9x leverage while maintaining security and decentralization.

The protocol operates without Recovery Mode, ensuring borrowers always benefit from high LTV (loan-to-value) ratios. BOLD is minted on borrow and redeemed 1:1 for collateral, protected by a Stability Pool and BOLD/ETH pool to mitigate liquidation and peg risks. This design addresses the limitations of V1, such as Recovery Mode delays and limited collateral types, while introducing restaking support for yield-bearing LSTs like stETH, rETH, and cbETH.

Motivation: DeFi lending remains inefficient, with high collateral requirements and variable interest rates. V2 solves this by offering interest-free loans (one-time fee model) and LST integration, allowing users to earn yield on collateral while borrowing. The protocol is fully immutable, with no governance tokens controlling parameters, ensuring long-term trustlessness.

1.1 Protocol Overview

V2 consists of core contracts: TroveManager (manages borrowing positions), BorrowerOperations (user interactions), StabilityPool (peg protection), and BOLDToken (stablecoin mint/burn). LSTs are wrapped for collateral use, with oracles for price feeds from Chainlink and Uniswap V3.

4. Functionality and Use Cases

4.1 Borrowing

Users deposit ETH/LST to open a Trove, mint BOLD at 110% CR. Fee = 0.5% of debt.

Debt = Collateral Value / 1.1

Use case: Leverage ETH for trading while earning stETH yield.

4.2 Redemption

Anyone can redeem BOLD for collateral at $1 peg. Stability providers earn from redemptions.

4.3 Stability Pool

Stake BOLD to absorb liquidations and earn LQTY rewards.

5. Tokenomics

LQTY: 100M total supply. 44% community rewards (emissions over 5 years). Stakers earn 50% of borrowing fees + 100% of redemption fees.

BOLD: Supply expands/contracts with borrowing/repayment. Peg maintained by redemption + Stability Pool.

6. Risks and Mitigations

6.1 Liquidation Risk

Mitigated by 110% CR threshold and Stability Pool gas compensation. Probability low in bull markets.

Liquidation Penalty = 0.5% of collateral

6.2 Peg Stability Risk

Adaptive fees (spike/decay) encourage redemptions if peg >$1.01. Historical V1 peg deviation <0.5%.

6.3 Smart Contract Risk

Multi-audit by Trail of Bits, Sigma Prime. Immutable core; upgradeable periphery only.

6.4 Oracle Risk

Chainlink + Uniswap V3 TWAP oracles; fallback to manual circuit breaker.
